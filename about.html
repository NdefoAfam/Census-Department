About The Department

Ag. DIRECTOR CENSUS DEPARTMENT.

The current acting Director Mr. Afegbua Bello Abubakar is coming with the background of having served as, LGA Comptroller; TA Director Lagos Liaison office; TA Director Census 2006 PHC; State Coordinator 2006 PHC Jigawa State; PES Coordinator, 2006 PHC, Kaduna State; NDHS 2018 Coordinator, Nasarawa & Niger States; State Director, Edo State and Director Data Administration, Bayelsa, Delta and Edo States. Undoubtedly, these qualities position him as an excellent match for the role.He is also an Alumnus of the International Program Census, Washington DC on Census Planning and Management.


Census Department is one of the premier units in the Commission and is sometimes referred to as the heart of the Commission. The mission goal of the department is to be an outstanding collector and provider of timely, relevant and quality socio-demographic data about Nigeria. In particular the mandate of the department includes Plan and execute censuses,Design and execute the inter-censal surveys and other socio-demographic surveys that may be referred to the departmentResponsible for the supervision, monitoring and reporting on fieldwork connected with census enumeration, socio-demographic survey, inter-censal surveys, Prepare census and survey instruments, tabulation plan, Establish a population database for the dissemination of demographic information about Nigeria, and Undertake other duties that may be referred to by the Commission.


PERSONNEL

The overall staff strength of the department nationwide is currently about 512 personnel. At the headquarters, the department has 73 staff, 10 of whom are directorate staff while 58 are non-directorate staff.
